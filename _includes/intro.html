Our goal is simple: we are technologists that are and want to continue to be involved with the local community. With the ample hack space that we currently have — in-office viking table, shared coworking space — we have room to host meetup groups, hack nights, Nashville Software School study sessions, etc.

We have room to host ~25-35 people without breaking a sweat, which is difficult to find in general in Nashville. On top of that, we try to capture a bit of the GitHub spirit combined with the BNA team to have a place that reflects a nice place to want to work.

If you're ever in Nashville, know that you have a space to hack, friends to 🍺 with, and of course, that 🔥 🐔 and 🌆 🎵 to soothe your soul.
